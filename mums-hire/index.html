We are Hiring!

MumsVillage is growing rapidly so we need dynamic and hard-working individuals to join this exciting Tech Start-up. Our Co-Founders come with a wealth of experience in the International Technology and Marketing space. MumsVillage is committed to helping Mothers in Africa, and our focus is on developing content and business partnerships that have a significant impact on the lives of Kenyan Mums.

Mums Village Nairobi Careers Internship Opportunity

We are looking for a select few of talented individuals to join our mission as Interns. If you are interested in a fun, challenging and constantly varied work opportunity, Apply. If you are a self-starter, able to work closely with our team of Villagers and remotely under minimal supervision, Apply. We encourage applicants who are ambitious, reliable, courteous, committed and creative. Submit your CV to hello@mumsvillage.com.

MumsVillage is a vibrant online destination dedicated to enriching the lives of mums and mums-to-be in urban Kenya. Our Co-Founders created it as a home for Kenyan Mums to relax and express themselves among a community of like-minded friends. In this village you will find sympathetic Doulas, Doctors, Nutritionists, Fitness experts and Fellow Mothers.

The team

As the Team behind Nairobi's definitive online community for mothers, we are delighted to bring the MumsVillage experience to you. We are dedicated to giving you all the support you need as a Mum or a Mum-to-Be.

Isis Nyongo

(CEO/Co-Founder/Mum-in-Chief)

Isis is a media and technology leader in Africa who has held leadership roles at Asphalt & Ink, InMobi, Google and MTV. She has made her mark scaling media and digital businesses across the continent over the past decade. Isis specialized in mobile and local content partnerships at Google and led MTV's commercial entry into Africa. Isis holds degrees from Stanford University and Harvard Business School. Isis has been named a Young Global Leader by the World Economic Forum and one of Africa's most Powerful Women by Forbes. She serves on the board of Kenya's first MVNO, Finserve (an Equity Bank Group subsidiary).

Delauno Hinson

(CFO / Co-founder)

Delauno brings over 15 years of experience in public and private equity investing, strategy, entrepreneurship and risk management to MumsVillage. Formerly an Africa-focused venture capitalist, Delauno spent extensive time evaluating and investing in early stage companies across the continent, as well as working closely with entrepreneurs on operational, financial and strategic development. Delauno has also spent extensive time as a global public equities investor with a focus on emerging markets covering sectors including: Consumer Products, Technology, Industrial Manufacturing, Payment Services and Transportation. Delauno graduated from Howard University, Magna Cum Laude, and earned an MBA from Harvard Business School.

Millicent Muigai

(Product Manager)

Millicent is responsible for the product development for the Information Centre. She creates essential tools and guides for Mumsvillage Members to help them succeed in their journey of motherhood. She also works with business owners to establish and develop new opportunities to use the Mums Village platform to grow their businesses. Prior to joining MumsVillage, Millicent worked as a project coordinator with Google Kenya, assisting Kenyan business owners grow their online presence via Google+. She earned her Business degree in Business and Information Technology from Strathmore University in 2008..

Patricia Wanjala

(Editor)

Patricia has a strong track record in producing content targeted to Urban African women. Her work has featured in most of Kenya's major publications including newspapers, magazines, and books. A Liberal Arts honors Graduate from the University of the State of New York, Patricia has worked with local and international clients, including the Ministry of Information under the Government of Kenya. When she is not playing with words, Patricia is usually experimenting with recipes and volunteering with her daughter. She also runs a Natural Hair online community of close to 15,000 members.

Sonia Jones

(Community Manager)

Sonia handles Communities and Social Media Manager for MumsVillage. She is responsible for all of MumsVillage social media platforms. Sonia curates content to help mothers engage, interact and offer support to each other as they raise happy, responsible children. Prior to joining MumsVillage, she worked as a consultant for a Project Management firm. In this role Sonia was involved in several successful projects for large companies including Safaricom, Britam, World Health Organization and Population Services Kenya. She holds a Bachelor's Degree in Business and Information Technology at JKUAT.
