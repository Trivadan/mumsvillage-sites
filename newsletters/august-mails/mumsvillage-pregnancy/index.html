Hello Mums (and Dads) -to-be

We're always excited to hear about a new pregnancy and have been working hard to provide you with articles and resources. Our pregnancy-related content is developed by local mums and experts so that you have regular access to information that's grounded in the reality of becoming a mum in Nairobi and other parts of Kenya. We take pride in discovering the latest prenatal yoga class or ideas on African baby shower games - and sharing it with you.

We'd love to hear from you - please email us at hello@mumsvillage.com if you have any content requests and/or are interested in receiving weekly pregnancy-specific emails.
